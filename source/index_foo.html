About Me

Patrick Sinclair is a web developer based in London, England.

I am a technical lead at Sidekick Studios.

I was a principal software engineer at the BBC, working on the Radio and Music sites. I also spent a year with Radio 1 & Radio 1Xtra Interactive.

I was previously a researcher at the Intelligence, Agents and Multimedia Group and the Learning Societies Lab at the University of Southampton, where I did a PhD on Hypermedia and Augmented Reality.
